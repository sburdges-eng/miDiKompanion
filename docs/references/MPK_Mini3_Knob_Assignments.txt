MPK MINI 3 KNOB ASSIGNMENTS - PRO ENGINEER SETUP
===============================================

KNOB MAPPING (Default CC Messages):

Knob 1 (CC 70): TRACK VOLUME      - Master level control
Knob 2 (CC 71): PAN               - Stereo positioning
Knob 3 (CC 72): FILTER CUTOFF     - Synth/EQ sweep
Knob 4 (CC 73): FILTER RESONANCE  - Tone shaping
Knob 5 (CC 74): ATTACK            - Envelope control
Knob 6 (CC 75): RELEASE           - Envelope tail
Knob 7 (CC 76): REVERB SEND       - Space/ambience
Knob 8 (CC 77): DELAY SEND        - Echo/timing FX

LOGIC PRO SETUP - Controller Assignments:
1. Logic Pro > Control Surfaces > Controller Assignments
2. Click Learn Mode
3. Move knob on MPK mini 3
4. Click parameter in Logic to control
5. Repeat for all 8 knobs

VOCALS:
  Knob 1: Volume | Knob 2: Pan | Knob 3: Pitch Speed
  Knob 4: Comp Threshold | Knob 5: Attack | Knob 6: Release
  Knob 7: Reverb | Knob 8: Delay

GUITAR:
  Knob 1: Volume | Knob 2: Pan | Knob 3: Amp Gain
  Knob 4: Treble | Knob 5: Mid | Knob 6: Bass
  Knob 7: Reverb | Knob 8: Amp Master

DRUMS:
  Knob 1: Kit Vol | Knob 2: Kick | Knob 3: Snare
  Knob 4: Hi-Hat | Knob 5: Room | Knob 6: Complexity
  Knob 7: Reverb | Knob 8: Compression

SYNTHS:
  Knob 1: Volume | Knob 2: Pan | Knob 3: Cutoff
  Knob 4: Resonance | Knob 5: Attack | Knob 6: Release
  Knob 7: LFO Rate | Knob 8: Mod Amount
