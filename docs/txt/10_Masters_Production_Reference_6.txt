10 MASTERS OF INTIMATE PRODUCTION
Reference Guide for "When I Found You"
=============================================================

1. RICK RUBIN - No reverb, vocal in your face, strip to essentials
2. JUSTIN VERNON - SM57, keep breaths/gasps, dark tone = intimacy
3. TONY BERG - Fingerpicked acoustic, minimal, vulnerability first
4. DANIEL LANOIS - Treatments, movement, atmospheric irregularities
5. NIGEL GODRICH - Real spaces, natural reverb, bare grit
6. BLAKE MILLS - Saturation during tracking, dynamics matter
7. TCHAD BLAKE - Compression as ambience, SansAmp, lo-fi warmth
8. SYLVIA MASSY - Keep imperfections, mistakes are magic
9. JOE HENRY - Record live/fast, windows open, sonic naturalism
10. T BONE BURNETT - Analog, mono, presence over fidelity

REVISED VOCAL CHAIN:
1. Noise Gate (preserve breaths)
2. 1176 Compressor (4:1, slow attack)
3. Tape Saturation (subtle)
4. EQ: HP 80Hz, presence 2-4kHz
5. NO REVERB or max 5%

REVISED GUITAR CHAIN:
1. High-pass 100Hz
2. Tape/Tube Saturation
3. Gentle compression
4. Slapback delay 50ms, 10% mix (instead of reverb)
