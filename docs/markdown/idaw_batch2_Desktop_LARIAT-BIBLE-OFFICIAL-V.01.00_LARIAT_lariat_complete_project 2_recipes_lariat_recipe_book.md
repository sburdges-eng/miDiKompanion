### GREEN CHILE BREAKFAST BURRITO
*Signature breakfast item with house-made pork green chile*

**Yield:** 12 portions
**Portion Size:** 1 burrito (14 oz)
**Food Cost:** $3.85
**Menu Price:** $12.95
**Margin:** 70%

#### INGREDIENTS:
| Item | Quantity | Unit | Vendor | Notes |
|------|----------|------|--------|-------|
| Flour Tortilla (12") | 12 | each | Shamrock | Steamed |
| Scrambled Eggs | 36 | each | Shamrock | 3 per burrito |
| Pork Green Chile | 48 | oz | Made in-house | 4 oz per |
| Hash Browns | 24 | oz | Shamrock | 2 oz per |
| Cheese Blend | 12 | oz | Shamrock | 1 oz per |
| Bacon | 12 | strips | Shamrock | Optional |

#### PREPARATION:
1. **Mise en Place** (15 min):
   - Pre-cook bacon until crispy
   - Shred cheese blend
   - Heat green chile to 165°F
   - Pre-cook hash browns on flat top

2. **Assembly** (2 min per order):
   - Steam tortilla 30 seconds
   - Scramble 3 eggs on flat top
   - Layer: eggs, hash browns, green chile, cheese
   - Roll burrito style, tuck ends

3. **Service**:
   - Cut diagonal
   - Serve with side salsa
   - Garnish with cilantro

**Hold**: Max 30 minutes in hot hold

*Southern comfort classic*

**Yield:** 10 portions
**Portion Size:** 2 biscuits with gravy
**Food Cost:** $2.45
**Menu Price:** $9.95
**Margin:** 75%

#### INGREDIENTS:
| Item | Quantity | Unit | Vendor | Notes |
|------|----------|------|--------|-------|
| Buttermilk Biscuits | 20 | each | Made fresh | Or frozen |
| Sausage Gravy | 50 | oz | Made in-house | 5 oz per |
| Breakfast Sausage | 3 | lbs | Shamrock | For gravy |
| Heavy Cream | 16 | oz | Shamrock | |
| Flour | 6 | oz | Shamrock | For roux |
| Black Pepper | 2 | tbsp | Shamrock | Coarse grind |

#### PREPARATION:
1. **Gravy** (20 min):
   - Brown sausage, break into small pieces
   - Add flour, cook roux 2 minutes

### THE ROPE BURGER
*House specialty double burger*

**Yield:** 1 portion
**Portion Size:** Double burger (12 oz meat)
**Food Cost:** $5.25
**Menu Price:** $16.95
**Margin:** 69%

#### INGREDIENTS:
| Item | Quantity | Unit | Vendor | Notes |
|------|----------|------|--------|-------|
| Beef Patty 80/20 | 2×6 | oz | Shamrock | Fresh ground |
| Brioche Bun | 1 | each | Local bakery | |
| American Cheese | 2 | slices | Shamrock | |
| Bacon | 3 | strips | Shamrock | |
| Lettuce | 1 | leaf | Shamrock | Iceberg |
| Tomato | 2 | slices | Shamrock | 1/4" thick |
| Red Onion | 2 | rings | Shamrock | |
| Pickles | 4 | chips | Shamrock | |
| Rope Sauce | 2 | oz | Made in-house | |

#### ROPE SAUCE:
- Mayo (1 cup)
- Ketchup (1/4 cup)
- Yellow mustard (2 tbsp)
- Pickle juice (2 tbsp)
- Garlic powder (1 tsp)
- Onion powder (1 tsp)
- Smoked paprika (1 tsp)

#### PREPARATION:
1. **Grill** (8 min):
   - Season patties with salt/pepper
   - Grill 3 min first side
   - Flip, add cheese
   - Cook to 155°F internal

2. **Build**:
   - Toast bun on flat top
   - Sauce both sides
   - Bottom: lettuce, tomato, patty
   - Middle: sauce, bacon
   - Top: patty, onion, pickles

**Service**: With fries or onion rings

### MARIA BIRRIA TACOS
*Braised beef tacos with consommé*

**Yield:** 20 portions (60 tacos)
**Portion Size:** 3 tacos with consommé
**Food Cost:** $6.25
**Menu Price:** $18.95
**Margin:** 67%

#### INGREDIENTS:
| Item | Quantity | Unit | Vendor | Notes |
|------|----------|------|--------|-------|
| Beef Chuck | 10 | lbs | Shamrock | Cut 2" cubes |
| Corn Tortillas | 60 | each | Shamrock | |
| Oaxaca Cheese | 3 | lbs | Shamrock | |
| Dried Chiles | 8 | oz | SYSCO | Variety |
| Beef Stock | 2 | gal | Made/Shamrock | |
| White Onion | 2 | lbs | Shamrock | |
| Cilantro | 2 | bunch | Shamrock | |
| Limes | 20 | each | Shamrock | |

#### BIRRIA SPICE MIX:
- Cumin (2 tbsp)
- Coriander (1 tbsp)
- Black pepper (1 tbsp) - **Use Shamrock!**
- Oregano (2 tbsp)
- Cloves (1 tsp)
- Bay leaves (4)

#### PREPARATION:
1. **Braising** (4 hours):
   - Sear beef chunks
   - Toast spices and chiles
   - Blend chile sauce
   - Braise beef 3-4 hours at 325°F
   - Shred meat, reserve liquid

2. **Service** (per order):
   - Dip tortillas in fat layer
   - Griddle with cheese inside
   - Fill with meat (2 oz each)
   - Fold and crisp edges
   - Serve with 4 oz consommé

**Hold**: Meat up to 3 days, reheat in liquid
