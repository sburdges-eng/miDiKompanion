## PACK SIZE CONVERSION GUIDE

### Understanding Pack Sizes:

**Shamrock Format:**
- `1/6/LB` = 1 container √ó 6 pounds = 6 lbs total

**SYSCO Format:**
- `3/6LB` = 3 containers √ó 6 pounds each = 18 lbs total
- `6/1LB` = 6 containers √ó 1 pound each = 6 lbs total

**Standard Can Sizes:**
- `#10 Can` = 109 oz (6.875 lbs)
- `#5 Can` = 56 oz (3.5 lbs)
- `#2.5 Can` = 28 oz (1.75 lbs)

**Quick Conversion:**
- 1 LB = 16 OZ
- 1 GAL = 128 OZ = 8 LBS (for water-based)
- 1 Case (varies) - always check
