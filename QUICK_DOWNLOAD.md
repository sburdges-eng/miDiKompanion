### macOS Security Warning

When you first open the app, you may see:
> **"Bulling cannot be opened because it is from an unidentified developer"**

This is normal for unsigned apps. **Solution:**

**Method 1 (Easy):**
1. Right-click (or Control-click) on `Bulling.app`
2. Click **"Open"**
3. Click **"Open"** again in the dialog
4. Done! The app will open normally from now on

**Method 2 (System Settings):**
1. Try to open the app (it will be blocked)
2. Go to **System Settings** â†’ **Privacy & Security**
3. Find the message about Bulling being blocked
4. Click **"Open Anyway"**

### iOS Installation

For iOS, you'll need a sideloading tool since this app is for personal use only:

1. **Install AltStore** on your Mac and iPhone: https://altstore.io/
2. **Connect** your iPhone to your Mac
3. **Open** AltStore on Mac and enable WiFi sync
4. **Drag** `Bulling-iOS-Unsigned.ipa` into AltStore
5. The app will install on your iPhone!

**Note:** Free Apple IDs require re-signing every 7 days (automatic with AltStore running).
