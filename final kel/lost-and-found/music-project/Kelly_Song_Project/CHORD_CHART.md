## FINGERPICKING PATTERN (Intro/Outro)

6/8 feel within 4/4, let notes ring:

```
String:  6   4   3   2   3   4   3   2
Finger:  T   i   m   a   m   i   m   a
Beat:    1   +   2   +   3   +   4   +
```

Play root (6th or 5th string) on beat 1, then arpeggiate up and back.

---

## STRUMMING PATTERN (Verses)

Sparse, down-focused:
```
Beat:    1       2       3       4
Strum:   D       -       D       d u
         ↓               ↓       ↓ ↑
```

Let chords breathe. Space is part of the song.
